We’re delighted to announce the results of the 2025 IIIF Consortium [Executive Committee](https://iiif.io/community/consortium/consortium_committees/#executive-committee) election. One new institution, [Indiana University,](https://www.iu.edu/index.html) will join the committee to serve a 3-year term. [The University of Edinburgh](https://www.ed.ac.uk/) will continue their service on the Executive Committee with a 4-year term. [The National Gallery of Art](https://www.nga.gov/) and [Northwestern University](https://www.northwestern.edu/) will continue their service for 3-year terms.

They join the following institutions in serving on the Executive Committee:

* Bibliothèque national de France 
* The British Library
* Cornell University
* Europeana
* Harvard University
* J. Paul Getty Trust
* The University of Leeds
* Stanford University
* Yale University

For information about joining the Consortium, which makes an institution eligible to run in annual elections for Executive Committee member seats, [take a look at our membership benefits page](https://iiif.io/community/consortium/join/).
