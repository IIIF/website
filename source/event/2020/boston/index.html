Why Join Us About the Conference

Great Speakers

Embraced by a growing number of the worldâ€™s leading research and cultural heritage organizations, IIIF provides an open framework for organizations to publish their image and audio/video-based resources, to be viewed, cited, annotated, and compared by any compatible viewing application.

New People

These events will be valuable for staff from cultural heritage and STEM institutions, repository and collection managers, software engineers, and anyone engaged with image-based and A/V resources on the Web and in-person experiences. If you have not been involved with IIIF in the past, these events are a great opportunity to quickly get up to speed and understand the community and its benefits, particularly the IIIF Showcase. For more experienced attendees it is a great place to get the pulse of the community and see the latest and greatest developments.
